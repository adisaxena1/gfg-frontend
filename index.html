About Our Chapter

GFG-RKGIT drives peer learning: workshops, weekly coding practice, and open-source mentorship. We help members ship projects and improve problem solving.

Who We Are

GFG-RKGIT is a student-led community that helps students learn, code, and grow together. We conduct sessions, organize hackathons, and guide beginners into the world of development.

Activities We Do

Classes & Workshops

Weekly coding classes and workshops where students learn web development, programming, and problem solving through hands-on practice.

Hackathons

Our society organizes hackathons that encourage creativity, teamwork, and innovation among students.

Events & Seminars

We host tech fests, guest talks, and events that help students learn from industry experts and build connections.
